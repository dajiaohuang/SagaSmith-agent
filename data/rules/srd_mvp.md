# Playing the Game

## Attack Rolls

When you make an attack, roll a d20 and add the appropriate attack modifier. If the total equals or exceeds the target's Armor Class, the attack hits.

## Ability Checks

For an ability check, roll a d20 and add the relevant ability modifier. The Dungeon Master sets the Difficulty Class. A total equal to or greater than the DC succeeds.

## Advantage and Disadvantage

When you have advantage, roll two d20s and use the higher roll. With disadvantage, use the lower roll.

## Resting

A long rest restores a character's hit points to their maximum. A short rest allows a character to spend Hit Dice.
